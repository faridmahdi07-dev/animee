Yuji Itadori

Main character who fights curses after swallowing a cursed finger.

Satoru Gojo

The strongest sorcerer who mentors Yuji and protects students.

Nobara Kugisaki

Talented student and ally of Yuji in battling curses.

Powers & Techniques

  • Cursed Energy: Spiritual energy that powers attacks and defensive techniques.
  • Divergent Fist: Yuji’s special attack that hits twice in succession.
  • Limitless: Gojo’s technique manipulating space to create barriers and attacks.
  • Resonance & Straw Doll Technique: Nobara’s cursed technique using voodoo-like dolls and nails.

Fun Facts

  • The manga started in 2018 and quickly became one of the most popular shonen series.
  • Yuji’s character design and personality are inspired by classic shonen protagonists but with darker twists.
  • Gojo Satoru is widely recognized as one of the most popular anime characters globally.
  • The anime adaptation is praised for its high-quality animation and fight choreography.
  • Fans love analyzing cursed techniques and ranking characters based on power levels.
