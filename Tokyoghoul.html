Introduction

Tokyo Ghoul is a dark fantasy story set in Tokyo, where humans coexist with ghouls—creatures that survive by eating human flesh. Kaneki Ken, a regular college student, becomes a half-ghoul after a deadly encounter and struggles to balance his human side with his new ghoul identity.

Main Characters

Kaneki Ken

The main protagonist, transformed into a half-ghoul. Struggles with identity and survival.

Touka Kirishima

A ghoul who works at Anteiku café and helps Kaneki adapt to the ghoul world.

Tokyo Ghoul:re

Continuation of the story where Kaneki faces new enemies and challenges.

Factions & Organizations

The world of Tokyo Ghoul has several groups:

  • CCG: Investigative agency hunting ghouls.
  • Anteiku: Café run by friendly ghouls helping humans and ghouls coexist.
  • Clowns & Aogiri Tree: Dangerous ghoul factions with hidden agendas.

Fun Facts

  • Tokyo Ghoul manga ran from 2011 to 2014, and Tokyo Ghoul:re from 2014 to 2018.
  • Kaneki's iconic white hair appears after intense trauma in the story.
  • The series inspired two anime seasons, several OVAs, and a live-action movie.
  • Fans often debate Kaneki’s choices and his moral conflicts.
